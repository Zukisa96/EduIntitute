What We Offer:

Counselling

We offer in-person and telehealth counseling as well as referrals. Visit our Counseling Services page to learn more.

Counseling Services provided include but are not limited to:

• Meeting with a Mental Health Counselor

• Evaluation and Referral Services

• Resource Connection

• Health Education + Groups

Scope of Care

In the Evaluation & Referral Services Clinic (EARS), students can meet with a counselor who can help them get connected to counseling services on-campus or in the community.

Supporting Concerns of Mental & Physical Well-Being

If you are concerned for a student's well-being, there are a variety of resources on and off campus that may provide support. Please visit our Resources pages to explore options

If you are concerned about specific behaviors being exhibited that are concerning, disruptive, and/or damaging, please visit Reporting Concerns webpage.
