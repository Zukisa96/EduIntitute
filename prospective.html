Welcome To EDUINSTITUTE

Since our establishment, we have championed independent thought by attracting and nurturing the world's best minds.

EDUINSTITUTE is a diverse community of world-class academics, students, industry links, external partners, and alumni. Our powerful collective of individuals and institutions work together to explore new possibilities.

EDUINSTITUTE prides itself in having a Experienced, skilled staff members. In addition to our in-house capabilities, we have established partnerships and agreements with other organisations to draw on additional skills where required.

AT EDUINSTITUTE,

Our students are directly involved in research, equipping them for the future and inspiring a lifelong curiosity. We teach our students how to think, not what to think.

EDUINSTITUTE is taking on some of the biggest challenges of our time, and making vital contributions to the public good. Discover more about our strategic goals, key statistics and history.

Our strategy is based on the following principal themes:

  • • Academic leadership grounded in intellectual excellence
  • • A leader in the integration of research and education, underpinning an inspirational student experience
  • • Addressing societal challenges through our disciplinary excellence and distinctive cross-disciplinary approach
  • • An accessible, publicly engaged organisation that fosters a lifelong community
  • • Delivering impact through a network of innovative international activities, collaborations and partnerships.

Vision

Our distinctive approach to research, education and innovation will further inspire our community of staff, students and partners to transform how the world is understood, how knowledge is created and shared and the way that global problems are solved.

Mission

EDUINSTITUTE is a diverse intellectual community, engaged with the wider world and committed to changing it for the better; recognised for our radical and critical thinking and its widespread influence; with an outstanding ability to integrate our education, research, innovation and enterprise for the long-term benefit of humanity
